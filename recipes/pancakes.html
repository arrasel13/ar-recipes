Mom's Potato Latkes

Latkes (potato pancakes) are a must-have at Hanukkah, but are really wonderful any time of the year! This is my mother's recipe, which is honestly the best potato latke I've had.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups shredded potato
  • ¼ cup grated onion
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 6 saltine crackers, or as needed, crushed
  • ½ teaspoon salt, or more to taste
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • ½ cup vegetable oil, or as needed
